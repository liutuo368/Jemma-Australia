Why Jemma

Jemma Australia is an all in one solution that provides services to facilitate a smooth relationship between tradies and customers.

How it works?

Tradie Customer

Publicly listed profile to showcase your qualifications and experience.

You're great on the tools, we know that. Let us help you with the online stuff, which is what we're great at.

More people every day want to easily find and order services online. With your brand and experience freely available to the public we'll give you more exposure for your business through a professionally designed, easy to read profile, ensuring customers know how great you are, without having to contact you and interrupt the job you're on.

Ratings and reviews

Customers are relying on ratings and reviews increasingly every day. Whether it be for food, entertainment, electronics, mostly everything. They are quickly becoming a deciding factor on who and what people choose to use and purchase.

This is your opportunity to let people know how good you are and we want to give you that opportunity by letting the customers tell everyone else why they should hire you.

Public calendar to enable customers to see when you're available.

How many phone calls do you take each day asking you when you're available? How many times do you answer the same questions each day and have to check your calendar?

Your profile will feature your calendar which will show customers when you're available. To ensure confidentiality only the times and dates will be visible to the public, however as a member of Jemma this is your tool to see the details of upcoming and previous work. Only you can see those details in case you need to check what you're doing later. You can also block out dates and times for those times when you need a holiday, or something.

Just let us know when you're available and we'll do the rest.

Pictures of your work for you to showcase your skills.

If your trade involves custom designs and fabrication then you'll have the opportunity to upload pictures of your previous completions. Whether it be a fence, landscaping or any job you'd like to showcase, Jemma will give you the freedom to not only show off your work but also give the customer some ideas as to what they want you to do for them.

A customer needs a fence but doesn't know what they want? Just wait until they see your portfolio!

Financial security through escrow payments.

Most people pay what they owe however we're also aware that some don't.

Even though this is only a small percentage of commissioned work, it's still a lot of time and money spent chasing up invoices. You don't need that hassle so Jemma provides an escrow service where the agreed upon amount for commissioned work is held upfront in a trust account until the works are completed.

Put simply, the customer books you in, you both agree on a set amount and estimated timeframe for the job and the customer deposits that amount in the trust account. This money is held while you complete the job, giving you the confidence that as long as the job is completed satisfactorily, you'll get paid.

Once both parties are happy then the customer signs off through the website/app and the amount owed is released to you and an invoice is issued to both parties.

No more chasing up invoices, you've got more important things to do.

Instant payments upon completion.

Payment cycles are a pain, we get it. You did the job so why should you have to wait a few weeks to be paid for it? It's a hassle to keep track of and it makes your bookkeeping more stressful than it should be.

With Jemma's escrow and instant sign-off features you'll get paid now because that's when you deserve to get paid.

No more interruptions.

Say goodbye to those random phone calls every day while trying to finish work for someone else. When the public has access to your profile, qualifications and skillset, you won't need to answer those questions.

With access to your calendar customers can see when you're available and book you within minutes. No more phone calls interrupting you while you work, meaning jobs are completed earlier and you're not answering the same questions over and over and over and...

Work/life balance.

Our research indicates that tradies spend too much time filling out paperwork, invoicing and other admin related duties.

If you're wondering "why has this not been automated yet?", it has. You're looking at it.

Jemma was specifically designed to take the burden from you. Admin, taxes and other paperwork are a hassle that you don't need, but they're necessary for your business to survive. So let us do the hard work for you because that's what we do.

You're great with the tools and we're great with the computery stuff.

Meanwhile, take that extra time you'll have to spend time with your family, or playing sport, or taking on extra work, either way it's more time for you.

What's in it for Jemma though?

What we offer is a system that will alleviate a lot of issues that the current business model suffers from. Our research indicates that everyone from both sides has some complaints about the current relationship between customers and tradies, and I'm sure you have a few tales to tell too.

However to put it bluntly, you're not a charity and neither are we. To continue to provide a service that we feel benefits everyone we still need to make a profit.

With that being said we aren't in this business to take advantage of anyone, our business model is specifically designed to make life easier and fairer for everyone involved and we feel that our pricing model reflects value and compromise on all sides.

We don't charge monthly or annual fees, meaning that you only pay for what you use. That's right, you don't pay unless you get booked for a job which means that your profile, with qualifications, portfolio, ratings & reviews are still there for the world to see, without paying anything.

To start with, the base level package offers: - Automated scheduling - Online profile - Ratings & reviews - Escrow & instant payment service - Portfolio pictures on your profile Cost - 5% of each job booked through Jemma

If you choose the Admin package you get all the features above plus: - License renewal reminders - Uploading of materials receipts for easier cost analysis
